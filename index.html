Negotiated Intelligence

Unearthing
Power Dynamics
in
Human-AI Co-Creation

Negotiated Intelligence dives into the curious dance between human imagination and AI collaboration. This exhibition uncovers the behind-the-scenes stories of co-creation, teasing out the power dynamics, surprises, and little quirks that emerge when humans and machines make art together. It’s a playful, interactive exploration of how agency, intention, and hybrid creativity collide, and sometimes conspire in AI-assisted creative work.
