Unearthing

in
Human-AI Co-Creation

Abstract

Negotiated Intelligence dives into the curious dance between human imagination and AI collaboration. This exhibition uncovers the behind-the-scenes stories of co-creation, teasing out the power dynamics, surprises, and little quirks that emerge when humans and machines make art together. It’s a playful, interactive exploration of how agency, intention, and hybrid creativity collide, and sometimes conspire in AI-assisted creative work.

Negotiated Intelligence is a study in how authorship and imagination are continually redefined in the shadow of algorithms. Here, creative archives are unearthed like archaeological strata, revealing the traces of human decisions entangled with AI. What freedoms does technology grant, and where does it erode our agency? Where does it expand the boundaries of creativity, and where does it quietly claim authorship? Often skipped-over in exhibitions, the mechanics of technology become the very subject here — a negotiation in which we repeatedly ask: who can we still be?
